# Outer Arm
The **Outer Arm** is galactic region #27 in the Milky Way. It is bordered by the Outer Scutum-Centaurus Arm, The Conduit, the Perseus Arm, Vulcan Gate, the Errant Marches, Aquila's Halo, and The Void.
