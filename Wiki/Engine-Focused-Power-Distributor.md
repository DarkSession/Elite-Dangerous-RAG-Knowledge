# Engine Focused Power Distributor
**Engine Focused Power Distributor** is an engineer modification that can be applied to Power Distributors. It increases engine capacity and recharge rates, at the cost of decreased systems and weapons capacity and recharge rates.

It is available in 5 grades, with the highest grade offering up to 60% increase in engine capacity, and a 44% increase in engine recharge rate.

## Availability

This modification can be applied by the following engineers, up to the maximum specified grade:

- The Dweller (Grade 5)
- Etienne Dorn (Grade 5)
- Marco Qwent (Grade 3)
- Hera Tani (Grade 3)
