# Normal Speed
Normal speed
 		 	 

**Normal speed** is the speed a ship travels in normal space, without activating the boost function. It's slower thanÂ Orbital Cruise.

The maximum normal speed of a ship can only be achieved by:

- Full throttle
- Four Pips in ENG
- Landing gear retracted
- Cargo Scoop retracted
- Flight Assist on

Different ships have different maximum normal speeds, currently ranging from 130 to 330 m/s.
