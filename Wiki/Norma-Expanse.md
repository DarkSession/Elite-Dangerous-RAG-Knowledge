# Norma Expanse
**Norma Expanse** is galactic region #10 in the Milky Way. It is bordered by Norma Arm, Arcadian Stream, Trojan Belt, Dryman's Point, Hawking's Gap, Inner Orion Spur, and Inner Scutum-Centaurus Arm.
