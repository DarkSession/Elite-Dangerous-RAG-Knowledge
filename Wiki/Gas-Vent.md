Gas Vents
 		 	 

A **Gas Vent** periodically emits gas from a planet or moon. The appearance is quite similar to Geysers, but the gas emissions are like a cloud rather than a geyser eruption. Crystals that form in the vicinity of Gas Vents can be harvested for Materials.
