# Hieronymus Delta
- *Not to be confused with Hieronymous.*

**Hieronymus Delta** is galactic region #25 in the Milky Way. It is bordered by The Abyss, Formorian Frontier, Newton's Vault, The Conduit, and Outer Scutum-Centaurus Arm.
