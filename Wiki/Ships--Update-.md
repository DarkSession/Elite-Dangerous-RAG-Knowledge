# Ships (Update)
- *Not to be confused with Ships.*

The **Ships Update**, also referred to as **v1.5**, is a free update that added five additional ships to Elite Dangerous, along with several new missions and encounter scenarios, and various fixes and improvements.^[1]^ Its release coincided with the release of Elite Dangerous: Horizons 2.0 on December 15, 2015.^[2]^

## New ships

- Asp Scout
- Federal Corvette
- Imperial Cutter
- Keelback
- Viper MkIV
