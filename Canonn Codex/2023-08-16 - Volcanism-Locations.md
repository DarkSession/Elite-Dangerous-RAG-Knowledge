## Volcanism Locations

Published: 16 Aug 2023 on Canonn (https://canonn.science/codex/volcanism-locations/)

Content: Have you been looking for a specific type of volcanism and just can’t seem to figure out where to find it? Fortunately for you, CMDR Felsen has put together the following lists that show what type of volcanism you can find on a specific body and inversely where to find a specific volcanism. Please note that this list was compiled in Elite Dangerous Horizons and might have changed with the release of Elite Dangerous Odyssey. If you find any discrepancy feel free to report it in the Canonn Discord and we’ll update this article.

Note that Rocky Ice Worlds currently have a low sample count.
