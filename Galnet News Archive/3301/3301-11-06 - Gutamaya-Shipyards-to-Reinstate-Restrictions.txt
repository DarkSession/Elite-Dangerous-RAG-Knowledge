Title: Gutamaya Shipyards to Reinstate Restrictions
Published: 06 NOV 3301 on Galnet News
Content: Last month, Gutamaya Shipyards marked Arissa Lavigny-Duval's coronation by temporarily lifting the rank-related restrictions on all Imperial ships. With the official coronation period now over, the company has announced that the restrictions on its ships will be reinstated on Monday.
